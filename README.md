# RenewPass

[<img align="left" src="RenewPass/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/Icon-App-60x60@3x.png" hspace="20">](#logo)

This is the iOS version of RenewPass, an open-source, cross-platform project that keeps you riding! üöéüöÜ

The Android version is developed and maintained by Coffeeboys: https://github.com/Coffeeboys/RenewPass

RenewPass does the work of renewing your UPass for you. Enter your school credentials, and RenewPass will log in to your UPassBC account and request the latest available UPass, all with a single tap. ‚úÖ

Your school credentials are stored securely in the iOS keychain, which is encrypted with your iCloud password.

Supported schools (beta testers needed!):
- SFU (tested)
- UBC
- BCIT
- VCC
- KPU
- Emily Carr
- Douglas (tested)
- Langara
- CapU
